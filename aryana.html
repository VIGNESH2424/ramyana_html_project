At the time of the Mughal Empire in Medieval India, Ramcaritmanas(an epic poem) was written by Tulsidas in 1574.In verse 1.33.2 of Balkand, the first chapter of Ramcaritmanas, Tulsidas mentions 1631 as the date according to Vikram Samvat calendar, which is 1574in the Gregorian calendar or Common Era(CE). A composition of Avadhi dialect, the Raamcaritmanas belonged to the saguna form of the Bhakti movement(also called Bhakti kal or devotional period).
